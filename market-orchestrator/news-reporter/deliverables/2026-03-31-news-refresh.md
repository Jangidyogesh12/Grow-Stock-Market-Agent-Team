- A March 26 government notification cut the special additional excise duty on petrol from Rs 13 per litre to Rs 3 per litre and removed the diesel levy, prompting a relief rally in OMC stocks including IOC.

- Takeaway: this is the clearest fresh positive item in the watchlist because it improves near-term sentiment and partially cushions refining-marketing stress.

### HDFCGOLD

- Takeaway: sentiment remains driven more by ETF-rule interpretation and gold-price volatility than by issuer-specific developments.
